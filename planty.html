About Us

Get to know us more

Connect with us

Planty is an organization interested in agriculture, plants and roses, whether indoor or outdoor, and is also based on the principle of helping the environment .

  • Planty was founded in 2022 in the Kingdom of Bahrain by Fatima Ali, the owner of this project.
  • Plante is based on spreading the hobby of agriculture and passing it on to the current generation.
  • Planty sells plants, agricultural tools, and others, in addition to organizing projects related to agriculture. with the aim of reclaiming the land.

In addition, Planty was founded by a group of friends who share the same dream, in spreading and planting plants throughout the land, little by little until Planty became the reality of the dream they planted.

What is special about Planty?

Planty includes a group of specialists in the treatment of agriculture who have a high ability to teach our clients agricultural techniques and facilitate them for them, in addition to the fact that Planty has an innovative project idea, so it is difficult to obtain competitors who carry our principles, products and exceptional seals at nominal prices.
